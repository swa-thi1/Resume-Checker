Actionable Tips for ATS Compatibility

Use Standard Fonts

Stick to Arial, Calibri, or Times New Roman (10-12pt) to ensure readability by ATS software.

Avoid Tables and Graphics

ATS can't parse complex layouts. Use simple text-based sections instead.

Include Relevant Keywords

Scan the job description and incorporate exact phrases like skills and qualifications.

Save as PDF or DOCX

These formats are widely supported. Name your file professionally, e.g., "FirstName_LastName_Resume.pdf".
